The journey begins

- 23rd March 2021 I (Em1t) got an idea to make a host. There was way too much planning ahead, so I started gathering friends that would like to help me with my idea.
- 26th March 2021 Discord server created. B1tt and my brother joined.
- 27th March 2021 Em1t started working on the website. B1tt started planning back-end.
- 30th March 2021 Website was finished, but I decided to create a new version, as this version was not scalable.
- 19th April 2021 We nearly made business with a node provider, but we ran background checks and decided not to go with them afterall, due to the practices they used being shady.
- 22nd April 2021 B1tt decided to rewrite the back-end to a modular back-end.
- 1st March 2021 The main landing page was finally finished, after 3 versions that were discarded
